PHF Bamboo Rayon Sleep Sack

Designed for the perfect night's sleep, this sleep sack combines softness, breathability, and safety for your baby. Crafted from a unique 70% bamboo rayon and 30% cotton blend, it offers a luxurious feel that's significantly gentle on delicate skin compared to traditional cotton.

  • Bamboo rayon and cotton blend for natural softness and breathability

  • 0.5 TOG lightweight design suitable for all seasons

Why Choose This Sleep Sack?

Whether it's a warm summer night or a breezy spring evening, this sleep sack adapts seamlessly to your baby's needs.
