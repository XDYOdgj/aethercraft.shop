FIEMOL Baby Sleep Sack

A safe and cozy sleep solution for newborns and infants up to 6 months

The FIEMOL baby sleep sack is a perfect blend of comfort and practicality, designed to help your little one sleep soundly during the night. With a 2-layer fabric construction that combines breathable cotton with ultra-soft plush, it offers the ideal balance of warmth and ventilation, keeping your baby just right in any climate.

The 1.5 TOG rating ensures appropriate warmth for cooler nights, while still allowing for breathability. This makes the sleep sack suitable for year-round use, especially as an essential layer during colder months. The 3D plush dots on the exterior not only add a touch of luxury but also provide a soothing tactile experience, helping your baby relax and feel secure.

  • 2-Layer Design: Combines breathable cotton and ultra-soft plush for comfort and temperature regulation.

  • Machine Washable & Long-Lasting: Soft and durable fabric that retains warmth and comfort through multiple washes.

Why Choose FIEMOL?

Designed for parents who want a reliable and safe sleep solution, these sleep sacks are perfect for newborns and infants up to 6 months. Available in sizes 0-3 and 3-6 months, they’re built to last and provide a cozy, secure environment for your baby to rest and grow.
