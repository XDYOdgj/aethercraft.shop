Kaiya Baby Unisex Sleep Sack

Designed for the perfect night's sleep, our Kaiya Baby Sleep Sack is a must-have for parents looking to provide comfort and safety for their little one. Made from a premium blend of organic cotton, polyester, and wool, this wearable blanket offers the ideal combination of softness, durability, and breathability, making it gentle on your baby's sensitive skin.

With a GOTS-certified organic cotton base, this sleep sack is not only safe but also eco-friendly, ensuring a healthier sleep environment for your baby.
