Yoofoss Baby Sleep Sack

Soft, safe, and stylish sleepwear for your baby’s best night's sleep

Soft & Breathable 100% Organic Cotton

Our Yoofoss Baby Sleep Sack is made from 100% organic cotton that's gentle on your baby's sensitive skin. It's hypoallergenic, breathable, and designed to provide a comfortable sleep environment, ensuring your little one stays cozy without feeling restricted.

Safe & Easy 2-Way Zipper

The 2-way zipper design makes it easy to change your baby’s diapers without waking them up. It’s also equipped with zipper protectors to prevent any potential jaw scratches, giving you peace of mind during those precious sleep moments.

Sleeveless for Optimal Temperature Control

Designed with a sleeveless style, this sleep sack allows for natural body temperature regulation, reducing the risk of overheating. It's perfect for moderate temperatures, keeping your baby warm yet comfortable all night long.

Secure Neckline & Loose Legs for Safety

The secure neckline and loose leg design help prevent any accidental slipping, ensuring your baby can rest soundly. This design also supports healthy hip development and promotes safe sleep practices for your little one.

Perfect for Every Season

With a 0.5 TOG rating, this sleep sack is ideal for spring, summer, autumn, and even cool nights. It's suitable for temperatures between 75-80°F, making it versatile whether your baby is in a crib, bassinet, or just taking a nap in a cool room.

Easy to Clean & Durable

Constructed to withstand millions of washes, the Yoofoss Sleep Sack maintains its shape and softness after regular machine washing and low heat drying. It's built to last while keeping your baby's sleep time worry-free.

Why Choose Yoofoss?

Give your baby the best start with our 0.5 TOG sleep sack designed for comfort, safety, and style. Whether it's for nighttime sleep or a quick nap, it’s the perfect wearable blanket for those who want to ensure their little one stays warm, snug, and secure.
